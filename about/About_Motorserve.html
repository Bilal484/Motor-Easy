CAR SERVICING

WHY MOTOREASY?

Motoreasy is owned by Insurance Australia Group Limited (IAG) and has been proudly helping Australians since 2007, by providing quality repairs and car servicing. You can rely on Motoreasy to provide you with:

  • Expert mechanics: From regular car servicing to major repairs, we have all the right equipment and expertise.
  • Convenience:Our wide network of car servicing stores are conveniently located across NSW and ACT.
  • Quality: All parts and repairs come with a minimum warranty of 12 months or 20,000km°.
  • Value: We have great prices and get your approval on all work, plus NRMA Members save 10%^
How can we help?

Our NSW and ACT Motorserve car servicing stores can provide you with:

  • logbook car service
  • eSafety/Rego check
  • car reliairs
  • brake services
  • air conditioning service and re-gas
  • clutch and transmission reliair services
  • radiator reliairs
  • child restraint fitting
  • pre-purchase vehicle inspections (selected stores)

We also have Motoreasy Assessment Centres located in NSW, Victoria and Queensland where you can take your vehicle for Insurance Assessments.
